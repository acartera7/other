Lets continue on my project to make a MicSpammer using Qt and WASAPI. Right now a lot of features are missing including proper selection of audio devices, playback and throughput into the mic and maybe eventual midi controls.

We currently have : AudioLoader.cpp AudioPlayer.cpp MicSpammerWindow.cpp FileBrowserWidget.cpp WasapiManager.cpp NumpadWidget.cpp and of course main.cpp.
